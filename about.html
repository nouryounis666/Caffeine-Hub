Our Story

Caffeine Hub was born from a deep passion for exceptional coffee and meaningful community. Our founders traveled across renowned coffee regions, from Ethiopia to Colombia, seeking beans with character and soul. Every cup we serve reflects that dedication to craft and authenticity. We have been brewing perfection for over 30 years and are proud Members of The Specialty Coffee Association.

Our Goal

We have been sourcing ethical beans for over 30 years and are Members of The Specialty Coffee Association. We craft brews big and small from single-origin pour-overs to full espresso-based drinks and we are certified Fair Trade coffee roasters.
